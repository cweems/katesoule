I work with people from all walks of life in my private practice. People often come to see me to explore the embodied origins of:

  • Chronic tension, aches and pains
  • Depression
  • The biological trait of high sensitivity. This trait is often present in artists and musicians.
  • Feelings of limitation in life situationally, emotionally, intellectually, or physically
  • Trauma including verbal, physical and emotional trauma, neglect, and intergenerational trauma
  • Difficulty creating or sustaining intimate relationships
  • Overwhelming emotions related to structural oppression
  • PMS and Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder
  • Difficulty feeling heartfulness, pleasure and joy
